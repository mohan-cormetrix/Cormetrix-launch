Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is CorMetrix?
CorMetrix is a healthcare and legal technology company that builds AI-powered solutions to help professionals make better decisions. Our platform turns complex data into structured intelligence that improves with every case.
How does your AI technology work?
Our AI technology combines machine learning, natural language processing, and expert-led design to analyze and structure complex information. It learns from each case to continuously improve accuracy and relevance, while always keeping humans in the decision-making loop.
Is my data secure with CorMetrix?
Yes, security is our top priority. We use enterprise-grade encryption, maintain HIPAA and SOC 2 compliance, and implement strict access controls. Your data is never used to train general AI models, and we maintain complete audit trails of all system access.

Product Questions

What is VerixAI?
VerixAI is our medico-legal solution that helps legal teams review medical records more efficiently. It extracts key information, organizes case files, and helps you create comprehensive, source-linked reports that stand up to scrutiny.
What is AtriAI?
AtriAI is our healthcare solution focused on left atrial appendage occlusion (LAAO) procedures. It streamlines case management by organizing patient data, automating documentation, and providing clinical decision support throughout the entire workflow.
Can your products integrate with our existing systems?
Yes, our platform is designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing EHR, imaging systems, and document management solutions through secure APIs. We work closely with your IT team during implementation to ensure smooth integration.

Pricing & Support

What is your pricing model?
We offer flexible subscription plans based on your organization's size and needs. We also provide per-case pricing options for some products. Contact our sales team for a customized quote tailored to your specific requirements.
What kind of support do you provide?
We provide comprehensive support including implementation assistance, training, and ongoing technical support. Our customer success team is available via email, phone, and chat to help you get the most out of our platform.
How long does implementation take?
Implementation typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on the complexity of your environment and integration requirements. Our team works closely with you throughout the process to ensure a smooth transition.
