Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of cases does VerixAI handle?
VerixAI is designed to handle all types of medico-legal cases, including personal injury, medical malpractice, workers' compensation, and disability claims. The platform is particularly effective for complex cases with extensive medical records.
How secure is your platform?
VerixAI employs bank-level encryption, SOC 2 compliance, and HIPAA-compliant data handling. All data is stored in secure, redundant servers with strict access controls to ensure your sensitive case information remains protected.
What is your pricing model?
We offer flexible subscription plans based on your firm's size and case volume, as well as per-case pricing options. Contact our sales team for a customized quote tailored to your specific needs.
How do we onboard?
Our white-glove onboarding process includes system setup, data migration if needed, and comprehensive training for your team. A dedicated customer success manager will guide you through implementation and ensure a smooth transition.
What is your uptime?
VerixAI maintains a 99.9% uptime guarantee, with scheduled maintenance performed during off-peak hours. Our robust infrastructure ensures reliable access to your case files whenever you need them.
